SHUTTLE INFORMATION

Shuttle Service will be provided from the hotel to the ceremony and reception.
Here is a few details on how shuttle service will work.

1

CHECK-IN AT THE NOTARY HOTEL

Check in at The Notary Hotel is at 4:00pm. If you plan to attend the ceremony you are welcome to leave your bags in a secure area with hotel staff. A shuttle will leave at 2:45pm to take you from The Notary Hotel to St. Richard's Church for the ceremony. You can check-in to your room when returning to the hotel after the ceremony. 

2

SHUTTLE TO THE RECEPTION

A Shuttle will leave the Notary at 6:00pm and again at 6:20pm to bring guests to Ballroom at the Ben for the reception. Shuttles will return guests to the hotel at the end of the night.

3

UBER

If you miss the shuttle the reception is a short Uber or cab ride away.
